About
Modern Painting Group

Modern Painting Group is a professional painting services company that specializes in exterior and interior painting needs. We have been in the industry for 15 years, offering high-quality service at affordable prices that assures customers of our expertise. As a locally owned and operated family business, we take great pride in providing a painting service that adds a personal touch to the customer’s experience.

Our Core Values

On-Time And On-Budget Service

We want to make sure that all our customers can afford themselves premium quality service that is convenient, hassle-free, and inexpensive.

Delivering On Your Promises

We always stand by our word to prioritize the promises we make to the customers about quality. Locally owned and operated, we make sure that the concerns and opinions of the customers are heard and taken care of.

Aim For Perfection

Our services do not just aim to be good, but to excel and exceed. That is why our work always shows a mark of perfection to customers.

Open To Growth

Modern Painting Group is constantly evolving to find ways to best suit the customers’ needs. We keep our communication lines open to customers and the experts working with us, as we value their opinion.

Why Choose Modern Painting Group

Why choose us over others? We believe our goal for perfection and excellence gives us the confidence and expertise to serve you best.

Our professionals are NACE & SSPC certified, bringing you their expertise and set of skills that always result in quality interior and exterior painting. With every job, our painters go into the fine-detailing of the interior and exterior surfaces to make sure they do a paint job that lasts.

Our company focuses on providing affordable painting solutions that will give you the feel and work of first-class servicing.

The customer is always our priority. That is why we will work closely with you to understand what you are looking for. From start to finish, we make sure that everything goes according to the customer’s satisfaction.

With all this in mind, we are confident that you will pick us for your next painting project.
