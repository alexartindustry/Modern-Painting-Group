Residential
Interior Painting

Our trained specialists at Modern Painting Group understand just how vital a paint job is. Nothing says home quite like a superior quality interior painting that perfectly coordinates with your sense of style. You just tell us your vision of home, and we’ll make it happen with a stroke of excellence!

Choose
Color Palette

The process of interior painting begins with color samples that show you an array of shades. We know how important color is when it comes to setting the mood and the vibe of your homes.

To make the decision easier for you, Modern Painting Group provides color consultation at no charge. We carefully examine the settings and the lighting to suggest the right colors that best suit you and the elements of your home.

Exceptional
Painting

The only way to get a job done is with the helping hand of specialists. The painters at Modern Painting Group have been in the business for years, garnering the knowledge of expert artists. We only use high-grade paint from Sherwin Williams & Benjamin Moore that is safe, vibrant, and guaranteed to last.

No Messes.
No Clean-ups.

No homeowner enjoys the mess of cleaning up after a paint job. Our painting experts come prepped and geared to do the job swiftly, with minimal mess. Your belongings will stay protected, including all switches and outlets.

At the end of every project, we do a full clean up of the site. Any and all waste is responsibly disposed of, making sure nothing is left behind – except a high-class interior paint. From start to finish, our goal is your complete satisfaction.

On-Time.
On-Budget.
Guaranteed To Last.

Our highest quality of service won’t break your wallet. Modern Painting Group affords residential homeowners top-notch interior painting that is budget-friendly and without hassles. You simply tell us when and where, and we’ll provide.
